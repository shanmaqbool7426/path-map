Build a modern AI-powered mobile app called **“PathPilot AI”** — an intelligent career & business roadmap assistant.

The app helps users learn, start, and grow real-world skills/businesses with personalized AI guidance, step-by-step roadmaps, daily tasks, evaluations, and practical strategies.

The goal is to solve this problem:

> “People want to start earning online or build careers, but they don’t know what to do first, what skills to learn, or what next step to take.”

The app should act like:

* AI mentor
* business coach
* career strategist
* accountability partner
* roadmap generator
* daily progress assistant

---

# Main Features

## 1. AI Career Roadmap Generator

User selects a category → AI creates personalized roadmap.

Example:
Ecommerce → Shopify → Pakistan Market → Low Budget → Beginner

AI generates:

* What to learn first
* Which products to sell
* How to find suppliers
* How to create store
* Marketing strategy
* Common mistakes
* Daily goals
* Expected timeline

Smart conversational AI assistant.

Capabilities:

* Answer user questions
